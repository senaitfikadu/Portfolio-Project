About

Cafe-Daily-record

This is a simple daily record website. created using front_end. will help your record sales and control your daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly budgets.

Inspiration for the project

Inspiration for the Project Have you ever struggled to keep track of your cafe sales and manage your budget?
I had a cafe and because of the manual record system, I failed! That's why
I decided to create a simple daily record tool that would help me restart and manage in a better way. As a student at Alx/Holberton School, I had the opportunity to work on this project as part of my portfolio.
However, the idea for the project came from my own personal experience and the need to have a better way to restart my cafe.
